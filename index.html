About Us

About Me

I, Jason Paul, am a professional computer teacher working in a school, handling higher secondary students and teaching them extensively about computers and programming languages. I am very proud of my students who have completed schooling and are now pursuing college courses. Their continuous encouragement and support to start, and their desire to learn and explore programming languages and the fundamentals of computers, made me so much marvelled. In addition, all my friends, family, and relatives motivated me to launch these wonderful computer-related courses that are helpful and supportive for all categories, from kids to adults. Here, I empower students to master new computer skills of their choice, and with routine practice, I make them rock solid to face any challenge boldly.

Our Mission

My mission is to offer interested kids who are schooling and college students pursuing their courses the chance to dive into computers and programming languages at the lowest possible prices, with the best-suited courses for selection and the utmost convenience.

Our Vision

With the motto "Learn & Grow," my vision is to unleash one's capability to the fullest extent of their creativity and potential in technology and computers. I aim to equip them to target their desired career successfully, lighting their path from darkness to brilliance, and inspire learners to discover new techniques they can explore online on their own.
